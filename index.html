Established in 2005, Inverse Path provides information security consulting to the most unique, challenging and critical sectors. We are an industry leader in securing hardware, safety critical embedded systems as well as every kind of software application or IT infrastructure. Our customers range from small businesses, to public and government organizations, to Fortune 500 companies.

Automotive We have been the very first group to publish research on vehicle security, since then we have been helping automobile and vehicle telematics manufacturers in securing their products.
Avionics We are one of the few industry leaders in avionics security, helping aircraft and avionics manufacturers to ensure that safety critical systems are protected from any hardware or software security issue.
Consumer Our deep knowledge of the convergence between hardware and software enables us to implement effective security and intellectual property protection for all kind of consumer electronics.
Enterprise Our team audits the IT infrastructures of the largest enterprise environments, uncovering vulnerabilities on mission critical servers and applications before malicious parties can exploit them.
Financial Being a recognized leader in credit card security, having uncovered new flaws in Chip & PIN systems, we secure entire banking infrastructures, from consumer cards to backend systems.
Industrial We specialize in securing safety critical infrastructures from the ground up. We have vast experience in auditing proprietary control systems, whether operating on air, land, sea or space.
